DataDog (DDOG)

Recommendation: buy · Target price: $120 · Time horizon: 3 months
  • The market has been punishing Datadog and other cloud platforms on the challenges the industry will face from the power of AI, but Datadog is well-positioned to gain from the AI disruption.
  • Datadog has come a long way in a short amount of time. A decade ago, it had a single offering for technology infrastructure monitoring. Today, it has more than two dozen products. It serves three "observability" businesses, each generating more than $1 billion in annualized revenue.
  • Datadog is an AI play. Enabling customers with the tools to build and accelerate AI workflows internally -- as well as monitoring and protecting its AI application agents -- is one of its fastest-growing businesses.
  • Datadog has now been GAAP-profitable for three consecutive years, and its guidance calls for record earnings in 2026.

Investing type: Moderate
